Hello!

Katrina has been referred to as "some sort of webdev wizard," but her skillset extends beyond just web design into the realms of 3D modeling and game design. She graduated from Towson University with a BFA in Digital Art + Design and is currently working as a Web Designer for Maryland Legal Aid in Baltimore, MD. She has a background in graphic design and also creates screen print designs for Elliott's Sportswear. In a group, she often takes on the role of project leader.

She loves technology, which has a major influence on her work. Her current obsessions are 3D printing and virtual reality. She has her own Da Vinci Pro printer for producing prototypes. She is currently a solo video game developer, and looking for a talented team to collaborate with.

If you would like to team up with Katrina or just have a good chat, send her a message at katrina.schmelter@yahoo.com
